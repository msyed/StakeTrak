Welcome to Staketrak

Automatically keep track of the stakeholders in your case, cause, or community.

Advantages of Using Staketrak

Staketrak fits into your existing workflow. Gone are the days manually entering notes into Excel spreadsheets and emailing different colleagues for info on stakeholders.

Automated Data Entry

Stop wasting time manually entering information into spreadsheets

Searchable, Centralized Database

We integrate databases from your different teams and offices into one platform you can search

Institutional Memory

Preserve institutional memory on stakeholders as you grow and turnover

Security

Ensure that nobody but your organization has access to your database of stakeholders

How Staketrak Works

Our technology automates the data entry process. Create profiles for hundreds of stakeholders in a matter of minutes.

Easy to use
Drag and drop your emails, memos, and meeting notes into our platform.
Instant Feedback
We pull all the stakeholders mentioned in your files, along with the contexts in which they’re mentioned, keywords associated with the stakeholders, and trends on which stakeholders appear together.
Intuitive Search
Search your database of stakeholders by name or a keyword, such as an industry or a location.
Work Together
We return stakeholders from documents submitted by any member of your team.
Relevant Information
Our output screen displays the file names in which the stakeholders are mentioned, the team member who submitted the files, the sentences in which they’re mentioned across different files, and relevant keywords.
Customize
Users can add notes on stakeholders through our comment box.
Quick Navigation
A dropdown menu allows you to categorize documents you submit by case or cause.
Fresh Filters
Select the filter option to see all the stakeholders associated with a specific case or cause.

About Us

Started in the fall of 2015, Staketrak is a team of Harvard students excited by the potential for technology and data science to make a positive impact. We hope to help governments, nonprofits, and other mission-driven organizations build relationships with those who hold a stake in their communities, cases, and causes. By building relationships, we believe we can help build economies.

We have been incubated by Harvard’s School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, Harvard Ventures, and the Harvard Innovation Lab.
