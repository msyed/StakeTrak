The Vision

We help organizations discover and profile the people in their data. For each cause or case, advocacy groups and lobbyists gather intelligenceâ€”thousands of pages of notes, emails, and memos. Two tasks ensue. First, they identify and research all the people they need to influence. Then, they influence them. Our software automates the first step, so humans can do what they do best: talk to other humans.

The Team

Rohan and Milton became friends in the fall of 2010 as high school freshmen. When Rohan told Milton about a problem he encountered, their passions for politics, strategy, and tech collided. At their core, they're just curious to see how computer science and statistics can help people make decisions.




Rohan studies statistics at Harvard. He grew up in Chicago and loves politics, data science, and competing in traithlons.


Milton is studying Applied Math at Harvard. He's a proud New Yorker and spends his free time solving puzzles.
